## For Users:

1. Download the app or login via doorlist.live and sign in using your name and phone number; these cannot be changed later, so make sure they are right the first time
2. Create an event on the app using the \+ in the dock or at doorlist.live/create
3. Check your organizations in the Profile tab of the app
4. Check your upcoming events in the Profile tab or in the Calendar tab of the app or in your feed on doorlist.live
5. Use your personalized QR code ("My QR Code") available via app or doorlist.live to scan into your events
