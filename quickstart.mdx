## For Users:
### 1. Download the app and sign in using your name and phone number; these cannot be changed later so make sure they are right the first time
### 2. Check your organizations in the Home tab
### 3. Check your upcoming events in the events in the Home tab or in the Calendar tab
### 4. Use your personalized QR code on the first tab to scan into your events
