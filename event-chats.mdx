---
title: 'Event Chats'
description: 'Admins of an event can create event chats to send messages to all invitees'
---

## How create an event chat:
1. Click the event tile. This could be from the Home tab or from the Calendar tab
2. Tap "Create Chat" to the right of "Event Chat"
3. Choose whether you want an announcement chat or a regular chat
 - Announcement chat: only admins can send messages
- Regular chat: all invitees can send messages
