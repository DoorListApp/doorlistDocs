---
title: 'Requesting Venmos'
description: "You can request Venmos from entire groups in a chat and track who has paid/who hasn't"
---

## Collecting Venmos:
1. Open the desired chat from the Chats tab
2. Tap the '+' in the bottom left corner
3. Tap 'Collect Payments'
4. Enter the Venmo handle that will receive the payments, the title of the payment, and the payment required
5. Tap 'Send'

## Tracking Venmos:
1. From the desired chat, tap the Venmo request
2. Tap 'Checklist' under the payment title
