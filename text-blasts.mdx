description: "Send SMS messages to all guests on the guest list - even those without the DoorList app"
---

## How to send a Text Blast

1. Tap on event tile

2. Tap on the megaphone icon on the top right

3. Type the message you wish for your guests to receive

4. Tap "Blast"

5. Check preview

6. Tap "Confirm and Send"

Note: You can send a *maximum of 5 text blasts*
