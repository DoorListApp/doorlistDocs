## How to send a Text Blast

1. Tap on event tile
2. Tap on "Text Blast"
3. Type the message you wish for your guests to receive
4. Tap "Blast"
5. Check preview
6. Tap "Confirm and Send"

Note: You can send a _maximum of 5 text blasts _- reach out to [help@doorlist.app](mailto:help@doorlist.app) if you need this limit increased\!
