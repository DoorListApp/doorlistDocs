## Method 1

### 1. Tap the Blue + Icon in the middle of the dock

### 2. Tap 'Create Group'

### 3. Give your organization a name and select your school.
#### If you are the first organization at your school, you will need to create a new school

## Method 2

### 1. Toggle to the home tab and select tap 'add group'


### 2. Select 'New Group'


### 3. Give your organization a name and select your school.
#### If you are the first organization at your school, you will need to create a new school
