---
title: 'Organization Groupchats'
description: 'Groupchats and their features'
---

*Organizations groupchats are automatically created when a group is made - all members are included. The member list will dynamically update as members leave/join.*

## Notable Features:
- Encrypted and secure
- Ability to send events
- Group Venmo requests
- Ability to disallow screenshots
- Ability to limit who can send messages
