title: "Organization Groupchats"
description: "Groupchats and their features"
---

_Organizations groupchats are automatically created when a group is made - all members are included. The member list will dynamically update as members leave/join._

## Notable Features:

- Encrypted and secure
- Ability to send events
- Group Venmo requests
- Ability to disallow screenshots
- Ability to limit who can send messages

## Create a Chat

You can also create chats with select friends or members of your group

1. From the Home tab, tap the chat icon in the top right
2. Tap the Box With Pencil icon in the top right
3. Select Friends, Contacts, \+1s, and more to add to your group chat
4. Tap "Create" to make the chat
